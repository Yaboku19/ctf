## Cookie Monster Secret Recipe

**Link**: https://play.picoctf.org/practice/challenge/469?page=2

**Difficulty**: easy

## Description

Cookie Monster has hidden his top-secret cookie recipe somewhere on his website. As an aspiring cookie detective, your mission is to uncover this delectable secret. Can you outsmart Cookie Monster and find the hidden recipe?
Additional details will be available after launching your challenge instance.

## How to solve

By looking at the site, and trying to login you will get to a page that will say

    Cookie Monster says: 'Me no need password. Me just need cookies!'

    Hint: Have you checked your cookies lately?

by copying the address of this page

    http://verbal-sleep.picoctf.net:<port>/login.php

and run 

    curl -s -D - -c cookies.txt 'http://verbal-sleep.picoctf.net:<port>/login.php'

you will save the cookies in `cookies.txt`

Than by running 

    cat cookies.txt

The output will be

    # Netscape HTTP Cookie File
    # https://curl.se/docs/http-cookies.html
    # This file was generated by libcurl! Edit at your own risk.

    verbal-sleep.picoctf.net        FALSE   /       FALSE   1762641840      secret_recipe   cGljb0NURntjMDBrMWVfbTBuc3Rlcl9sMHZlc19jMDBraWVzXzc4QjRDMzkwfQ%3D%3D

Where in the secret recipe there is something in base64

so by running

    echo cGljb0NURntjMDBrMWVfbTBuc3Rlcl9sMHZlc19jMDBraWVzXzc4QjRDMzkwfQ | base64 -d

the output will be

    picoCTF{c00k1e_m0nster_l0ves_c00kies_78B4C390}
